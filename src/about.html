— THE STUDIO · EST. 2018

A working studio.

Cinematic photography & film · Maryland

At Knoch Media, storytelling is at the core of everything we do. Through cinematic photography and film, we capture life’s most meaningful moments — the adrenaline of a sports event, the intimacy of a wedding, the essence of a brand.

Chapter 01 · The beginning

It started on a basketball court.

In 2023, Enoch picked up a camera to help shoot basketball for his college team. What was meant to be a favour to a friend became a calling. The first roll never developed into a portfolio — it developed into a way of seeing.

Chapter 02 · The philosophy

We don’t photograph weddings.

We attend them — quietly — and bring back what was beautiful. The best frames come from staying out of the way. Posed images have their place; the moments we’re after happen between them.

Chapter 03 · The craft

Film-first, even shooting digital.

Limited frames force intention. We approach every shoot with the discipline of a roll of film — every exposure earned, every composition considered before the shutter falls. The result is a body of work that holds up.

Chapter 04 · The work

A short list of clients.

We take on a small number of projects each year. Couples planning weddings that feel like films. Artists shipping records and music videos. Brands and athletes with a story worth a roll of frames.
